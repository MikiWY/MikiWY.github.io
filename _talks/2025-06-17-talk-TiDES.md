**Digital Economy & Dual-Chain Integration | Academic Research Project (Provincial-Level)**  
*Sep 2024 â€“ Present*

This ongoing provincial-level research project empirically investigates the relationship between digital economy development and the integration of industrial and innovation chains ("Dual-Chain") across countries.

*   **Core Empirical Research:** Developed a comprehensive digital economy development index. Utilized regression analysis on cross-country panel data to identify key drivers and impacts, deriving actionable policy implications from the findings.
*   **Working Paper:** Completed "*Does Digital Economy Development Facilitate Industrial-Innovation Chain Integration? Empirical Evidence from Global Cross-Country Research*." 
*   **Literature Review & Conference Paper:** Authored "*The Integration of Industrial and Innovation Chains in the Context of Digital Economy: Mechanisms, Challenges, and Future Directions,*" which was accepted and presented at the SSMI (Social Science and Management Innovation) conference.
*   **Academic Recognition & Dissemination:** The main research findings were presented at **The 2nd Tsinghua Digital Economic Tide Forum (TiDES)**, where the paper was awarded **"Outstanding Paper."** [Conference Report](https://sesu.scu.edu.cn/info/1059/11708.htm)
